Summaries

"IRead" is a platform that provides a summarized book app, allowing users to access the key takeaways of popular books in various genres such as self-help, business, and fiction. The app provides users with a quick and convenient way to get the gist of a book without having to read the entire thing.

"IRead" also has a feature that allows users to connect with other readers and discuss the books they are reading. This creates a community of like-minded individuals who can share their thoughts, opinions, and insights on the books they have read.

Public Chat

Provides a public chat feature that allows users to engage in discussions with other readers. The public chat feature on "IRead" enables users to connect with like-minded individuals who share their interests in books and reading. Users can participate in group chats, share their thoughts and opinions on books they have read, and exchange recommendations with other members of the community.
